SchoolSync: School Events Scheduler and Announcement Platform

In schools like Colegio De San Jose, events such as meetings, activities, holidays, and announcements are often shared through group chats, classroom announcements or word of mouth. While these methods work, they can lead to missed information, confusion about schedules, or last-minute updates that not everyone receives. As ICT students, we see an opportunity to improve this by creating a web-based school events scheduler and announcement system. This platform allows students, teachers, and staff to view upcoming events and important announcements in real time using their phones or computers.
